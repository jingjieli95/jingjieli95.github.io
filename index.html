Hey, thanks for stopping by Jingjie (a.k.a JJ)'s tiny place on the Internet. I am currently a Ph.D. student in computer engineering at the University of Wisconsin—Madison. I am advised by Prof. Younghyun Kim at the WISEST Lab and work actively with the Wi-Pi Lab, where you can find a more "serious" copy of mine :).

My research focus is human-centered computing. I design usable, sustainable, and privacy-aware interfaces for smart device users. I started my exploration from the field of personality computing during my bachelor study in Australia. Now in Madison, I have been working on a series of projects including user authentication, user privacy in interactive systems, and privacy notification. I also work with brilliant folks here to design power-efficient human-centered systems.

I enjoy building these tangible gadgets that would potentially make our lives better and more fun, even by just a little bit, to exhaust the daydreams hoarded up during the Wisconsin winter.
